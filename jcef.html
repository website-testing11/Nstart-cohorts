About JSEF

Jaipur Student Entrepreneurship Fest

Welcome to the 7th Edition of the Jaipur Student Entrepreneurship Fest - JSEF 2024! It's an annual event hosted by Nstart, India's first school-level incubation centre aimed at fostering young entrepreneurs to kickstart their startup journey. JSEF 2024 promises an exciting experience filled with innovation, creativity, and inspiration.

Reflecting on our past successes, where esteemed individuals like Ashish Chanchlani, Stebin Ben, Amit Crazy XYZ, Yashveer Singh, RJ Ravindra, Kirti Rathore, and many more have graced us as judges, we've witnessed a thriving hub for innovation. With over 2000+ students and 30+ institutions, attending each edition, JSEF has become a platform to celebrate entrepreneurship, inspire creativity, and champion sustainability for a brighter future. Welcome aboard to JSEF 2024!

Theme

The theme for this edition of JSEF is "Taking Actions for Sustainable Tomorrow." All submissions should align with this theme, showcasing innovative solutions that promote sustainability.

Team Composition:

Participants can form teams with a maximum of 4 members. All team members must individually register to participate in the competition.

Participant Eligibility:

Participants must be students currently enrolled in Classes 6-12.

Plagiarism:

All submissions must be original work of the participants. Plagiarism or use of unauthorized materials will result in disqualification.

Judging Criteria:

Entries will be evaluated based on criteria such as creativity, feasibility, innovation, presentation quality, and potential impact.

Disqualification:

The organizing committee reserves the right to disqualify any participant or team found violating the rules or spirit of the competition.
