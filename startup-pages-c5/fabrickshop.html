About
FaBRICK Shop

The FaBRICK Shop is a pioneering eco-conscious company that transforms fabric waste into sustainable slabs. We aim to tackle the global textile waste problem by repurposing discarded fabrics into high-quality, eco-friendly slabs. These slabs serve as the foundation for crafting stylish and durable furniture pieces, contributing to a more circular economy and reducing the environmental impact of the fashion and textile industries. Not only does this cut down on the waste in landfills, but it also reduces the dependency on wood to make furniture, which is a step towards decreasing deforestation.

At The FaBRICK Shop, we blend creativity with sustainability, turning what was once considered waste into valuable resources. Our process involves meticulously selecting and processing fabric remnants, which are then compressed, bonded using casein glue to form sturdy slabs and then coated in resin for a fine finish. These slabs are versatile and can be used in a wide range of applications, from modern furniture to unique architectural elements.
