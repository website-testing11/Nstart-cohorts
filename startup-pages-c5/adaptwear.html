About
Adapt Wear

AdaptWear was founded with the vision of bridging this gap in the fashion industry. Recognising the pressing need for inclusive clothing, AdaptWear is dedicated to creating fashionable, functional, and comfortable apparel specifically designed for disabled individuals. By combining style with practicality, AdaptWear aims to empower people with disabilities, enhancing their confidence and improving their quality of life.

Our Producs

AdaptWear's product line includes a diverse range of clothing options:

Product:

  • Adaptive Tops and Bottoms: Featuring magnetic closures, Velcro fastenings, and elastic waistbands for easy dressing and undressing.
  • Accessible Outerwear: Jackets and coats designed with extended zippers, pull tabs, and adjustable hems to accommodate different mobility aids.
  • Stylish Footwear: Shoes with wider openings, slip-resistant soles, and adjustable straps, ensuring both style and comfort.
