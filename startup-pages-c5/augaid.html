AugAid

AugAid is dedicated to revolutionizing accessibility and communication for the deaf community through cutting-edge AugAide technology. Traditional methods of communication, such as sign language and lip reading, can pose challenges in various social and professional settings. However, AugAid is poised to address these challenges with a transformative solution.

Our Products

AugAid introduces smart AR glasses designed specifically for the deaf. These glasses utilize advanced speech recognition and live transcription technology to provide real-time text of spoken conversations directly in the user's field of vision. Additionally, AugAid's smart AR glasses offer voice language translation capabilities, facilitating seamless communication across different languages.

Key Features:

  • Live Speech Transcription: Instantaneous conversion of spoken words into text in real-time.
  • Voice Language Translation: Enables communication in multiple languages without the need for an intermediary.
  • Accessibility Settings: Customizable features to suit individual preferences and needs.
  • DIY kits - in this we will be providing the cloth with dyes so the customer can dye their clothes in a personalised way.

All the items listed above will be eco-friendly and sustainable. None of them will harm the environment.
