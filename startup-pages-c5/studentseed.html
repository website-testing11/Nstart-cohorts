Student Seed

Our target audience, aged 18-25, seeks financial support and guidance toachieve their academic and career goals. These Individuals aged 18-25 are often in the early stages of their academic or entrepreneurial careers, characterised by their ambition and drive. They aim to excel in both their educational pursuits and business ventures, seeking financial support and mentorship to achieve their goals.

These individuals value community, personal growth, and professional development. They actively engage in various projects and seek platforms that provide resources and opportunities to support. We cater to those who value community, personal growth, and professional development. Our platform generates revenue through service fees and contributions from parents, institutions, and donors. We offer a user-friendly crowdfunding platform with secure transactions, progress tracking, and robust mentorship access. Our marketing strategy includes digital campaigns, partnerships with educational institutions, and community engagement.

Our Producs

Our product is a user-friendly crowdfunding platform designed to provide financial support and mentorship to individuals aged 18-25, helping them achieve their academic and entrepreneurial goals. The platform offers secure transactions, progress tracking, and access to valuable mentorship. Revenue is generated through service fees and contributions from parents, institutions, and donors. In addition, we focus on community building and professional development, providing users with the resources and opportunities to support their educational and business ventures.
