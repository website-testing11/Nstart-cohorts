Startup Marathon

J Curve

J Curve is a short immersive four-day startup marathon program aimed at accelerating the growth of students to give them exposure of being an entrepreneur. This would cover Entrepreneurship activities like Design Thinking, Team Building, Idea Generation, Pitch Deck Preparation, and Financials. This is a very quick and accelerated pace of learning and growth for the students to taste entrepreneurship.

startups Showcase

Makers Mela

Join us at the Makers Mela, where innovation meets opportunity under vibrant canopies. Discover the latest creations and entrepreneurial spirit as startups showcase and sell their innovative products!

Mentorship Sessions

Mentor's clinic

Mentoring Clinics are sessions offering personalized guidance by a panel of mentors or a mentor on specific topics such as: funding support, grant writing, business plan, IP etc. Innovators will get to interact one-to-one with the mentors.

Exploring Real-World Businesses

Field Visits

At Nstart, we take learning beyond the classroom. Our Field Visit initiative offers students a hands-on experience by exploring real-world businesses, industries, and startups. These visits provide practical insights into entrepreneurship, fostering critical thinking and inspiring young minds to innovate and build their own ventures.

Startup Workshops

Workshops

Our Workshops at Nstart provide students with essential entrepreneurial tools and frameworks. From Design Thinking and Business Model Canvas (BMC) to Lean Model Canvas (LMC) and Startup Models, these hands-on sessions empower students to develop innovative solutions, craft sustainable business models, and navigate the startup ecosystem with confidence.

Impactful Pitches and Presentations

Pitch & Persuade

At Nstart, we equip students with the art of delivering impactful pitches and presentations. Through our Pitching and Presentation initiative, students learn how to effectively communicate their startup ideas, structure compelling narratives, and confidently present to investors, mentors, and peers—turning great ideas into actionable ventures.

Founder's Meet-up

Founder Connect

The Founder Meet-up initiative connects students with experienced entrepreneurs and startup founders. These interactive sessions offer invaluable insights into the entrepreneurial journey, providing students with real-world lessons, mentorship, and networking opportunities to inspire their own ventures.

Tech Innovations

TechXplore

Our Technology Labs initiative introduces students to cutting-edge technologies like 3D printing, robotics, and AI. Through hands-on learning, students can explore the latest innovations, develop technical skills, and bring their creative ideas to life—pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in the world of entrepreneurship and problem-solving.
