Web Saheli

Web Saheli is a multilingual online anti-harassment and awareness platform that helps bring awareness about the problems underprivileged Indian women encounter while using online and digital services. The Web Saheli is a platform established on fair and moral principles, to educate marginalized women about maintaining a healthy presence online. To achieve this; we empower women to learn about modernism and to keep up with the glorious evolution of mankind in technology. Technology was invented and each day is growing to make our life easier and better. Web Saheli feels sad that all women are unable to enjoy the benefits technology has to offer. The feeling of grief shall be overturned into the feeling of joy, how? By helping the women in need, by educating and empowering them; which all leads to a better future and keeps Web Saheli happy as ever.

Schood

Schood ( School + Hood ) is a platform that gathers and connect learners. A peer to peer mentorship platform where you learn from your seniors, book sessions with our student mentors from our app and grow as you learn. We aim to provide practical knowledge, skills, and experience to school students through their seniors in order to overcome the huge mentor-mentee gap in our country. We take the best students from Class 11th and 12th (mentors) who have experience in various fields such as Leadership, Sports, Psychology, Public Speaking, Art, etc. and connect them with students of Class 6th and 10th (mentees), so that they can impart their knowledge to interested students and increase their knowledge at the same time.
